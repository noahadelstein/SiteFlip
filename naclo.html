NACLO

Washington University will serve as a host site for the 2016 North American Computational Linguistics Olympiad. This year the first round will take place 9:00am-12:00pm on Thursday, January 28. Contestants should come to the Psychology Building, Room 215B, on the Danforth Campus. (Directions to the Danforth Campus, information about parking, and a campus map can be found here.) Contestants should plan on arriving by at least 8:30am, but please don't arrive before 8:00am.

Schedule:

8:30am Welcome

8:45am Reading of the Rules

8:55am Distribution of contest booklets

9:00am Contest begins

12:00pm Contest ends
